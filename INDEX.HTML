About the Temple

Shri Rameshwar Mandir, also known as the Temple of Mahadev, is a divine place of worship dedicated to Lord Shiva in his form as Rameshwar — the one who brings joy to Ram. Our temple stands as a symbol of faith, spirituality, and community service.

With a legacy spanning over a century, the temple conducts daily Abhishekam, Rudrabhishek, and special Maha Shivaratri celebrations. Devotees from across the region visit to seek blessings for peace, prosperity, and liberation.

Daily rituals Panchamrit Abhishek, Bhajans, Aarti
Sacred Vatika Meditation hall & garden for devotees
Since 1876 Centuries of devotion & traditions
